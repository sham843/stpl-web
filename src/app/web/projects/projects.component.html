Rail Suraksha App

RailSuraksha CDMS(RPF Complaint & Disaster Management) Official App has been developed with the aim to take immediate action on emergencies and to resolve passenger's complaints in Central Railway Jurisdiction. In case of any emergency the user can directly call to 182 All India RPF Helpline provided through the application which will connect to the nearest RPF Control Room. A Panic button has also been provided to the user in the application so as to alert RPF and other enforcement agencies to respond to the emergency situation at the earliest. Any user can register a complaint related to the security issues in the Central Railway Jusrisdiction.

TRTI Web Application

'AadiVanmitra' online Portal has been developed with the objective of effective implementation of the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act 2006 and Rules 2008 and Amendment Rules 2012. This portal has been developed keeping in mind the objective of allowing the individuals / groups and villages / padas from the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers to file claims about Individual Forest Rights or Community Forest Rights related to forest land in a smooth fashion, to check the current status of the claims filed and to be able to get the forest rights title (certificate) without any hassles.
