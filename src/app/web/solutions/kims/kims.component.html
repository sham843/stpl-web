Introduction

Issue and receipt of weapons in a subunitâ€™s Kote, is an extremely time-consuming process, due to it encompassing entries of accurate figures, and simultaneously ensuring the issue to the right person. This process, on average, takes approximately 3-4 min per individual; in turn, a battalion individual would take around 3 hours for the complete issue procedure. While this time-consuming process is quite acceptable in peacetime, the ramifications of the same for a speedy mob of a unit are not acceptable.

Need for An Automated Kote Inventory Management System:
  1. Reduction in time in the issue of arms more so during mob, firing, and BPET.
  2. Issue of arms only to the person to whom it belongs to, to cut down on losses.
  3. Speedy generation of reports to monitor the issue and receipt, by the unit Cdr.

The Innovation

Shaurya Technosoft Pvt Ltd had devised an automated software-based system, which allows for the automated process of individual and GP issue/receipt of SAs from a battalion Kote the system is described as under:

A. Bar Coding : Each weapon (Rifle 5.56mm/CMG/LMG) and its mag have been barcoded. The bar code with its unique individual code is printed on each weapon and mag.
B. Bar Code Scanning : The barcode reader reads the barcode in a split second and displays all info with respect to the particular visual, his photograph, and his weapon; as a result, the Kote NCO instead of entering various figures in ledgers/ registers/ document, just scans the barcode to enter the read data fields for issue/receipt.
C. Finger Print Scanner: : The fingerprint scanner ensures that the individual digitally signs his issue/ receipt action which again is a very prompt process and hence obviates any wastage of time. It simultaneously renders authenticity, accuracy, andswiftness to the operation and also ensures that the weapon is issued only to the individual that whom it has been allocated to.
