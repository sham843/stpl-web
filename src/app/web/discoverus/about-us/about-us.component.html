About Us

Shaurya Technosoft Pvt. Ltd. is a Pune (India) based company. It is one of the fastest growing software company. We build software and provide advance technology services to our customers that add values to their business. We cater to the technology requirements of the Dairy Industry, Manufacturing Industry, Defense Sector, e-Governance, Mining Sector, Vehicle Tracking and Agricultural Sector. The other domains include Election Management, Inventory Management and Animal Husbandry. Shaurya Technosoft also undertakes offshore projects of software development.

We have successfully delivered a number of products which include Desktop Applications, Android Applications and Web Based Solutions. The end product is the convergence of a lot of research and development to cater to the needs of our clients which makes them achieve their full potential. We strive to achieve perfection and client satisfaction is prime important to us. We develop best-in-class software solutions to suit each client's requirement, keeping in mind the best practices in the industry.

We are privileged to be working closely with the Indian Army, Indian Railway and the Government of Maharashtra and it makes us proud to be part of Digital India Initiative.

We have a wide range of experience & expertise in domain of Desktop application, Web application & Android Application.
